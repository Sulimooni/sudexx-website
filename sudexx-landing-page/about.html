About Sudexx Diesel Repair

Your trusted partner for professional diesel engine repair and maintenance services in Dallas

Who is Sudexx Diesel Repair?

Sudexx Diesel Repair is a family-owned and operated diesel engine repair shop located in Dallas, Texas. We specialize in servicing 18-wheelers, box trucks, and commercial diesel vehicles with a commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Founded with a passion for diesel engines and a dedication to keeping commercial fleets on the road, our team of certified technicians brings decades of combined experience to every repair and maintenance job.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional diesel engine repair and maintenance services that keep your commercial vehicles running safely and efficiently, minimizing downtime and maximizing your business productivity.

What does Sudexx actually repair?

We provide comprehensive repair and maintenance services for all types of commercial diesel vehicles, focusing on the systems that keep your trucks running safely and efficiently.

Our Core Services Include:

  • Engine Systems: Complete engine diagnostics, repair, and rebuilding services
  • Oil & Maintenance: Oil changes, filter replacements, and preventive maintenance
  • Brake & Tire Services: Air brake systems, tire mounting, and wheel alignment
  • Computer Diagnostics: Advanced ECM diagnostics and performance optimization
  • Electrical Systems: Wiring, alternators, starters, and electronic components
  • Fuel Systems: Injection pumps, fuel lines, and fuel system maintenance
  • Transmission Services: Manual and automatic transmission repair
  • Fleet Services: Comprehensive maintenance programs for trucking companies

Specialized Equipment

We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and tools specifically designed for commercial diesel engines, ensuring accurate diagnosis and quality repairs every time.
